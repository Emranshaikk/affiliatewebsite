Why is a Light Jet heavily discouraged for the NY to LA route?

Physics. A Light Jet cannot combat the transcontinental headwind without halting mid-country (Kansas/Colorado) to refuel. This completely defeats the time-saving purpose of private routing. The baseline for your New York to Los Angeles private jet cost must factor in a Super-Midsize platform for genuine non-stop execution.

Why do brokers mandate Van Nuys (KVNY) instead of LAX?

Van Nuys strictly handles corporate and VIP traffic. LAX forces your private charter to sequence landing patterns directly behind colossal international A380s. Opting for Van Nuys preserves aggressive ground transitions.

How drastically do winds alter the flight block time?

Flying Eastbound (LA to NY) utilizes a tailwind, typically shaving 45-60 minutes off the flight. Flying Westbound (NY to LA) fights the jetstream, requiring stronger engines and higher fuel burn limits, increasing the required block time.
